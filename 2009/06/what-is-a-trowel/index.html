Dear fellow CEOs,

A question first: are any of the guys bothering with getting a tux for the Ivy Ball?

2nd: I’m planning on going to Lamma Island to hike. This should be really fun with great scenery and delicious seafood in the little villages there. We take a 40-min ferry ride from Pier 4 in Central, an easy hike for ~2hrs, 6km across Lamma featuring ancient limekilns and Japanese kamikaze grottoes and a restaurant specializing in pigeon dishes. RSVP if you are up for it.

WHEN: this Saturday, 9AM-2PM

Dear fellow CEOs,

For those of you who elect not to go Saturday, there will be a second hike on Sunday on the Dragon’s Back trail, which is on the SE part of HK Island. We take the MTR to Shau Kei Wan on the blue line, then bus 9 to Shek O Rd. The hike should be ~2hrs, 4.5km. Great views of the south side of the island, swimming at the beaches, and a visit to Shek O village.
